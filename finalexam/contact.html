Kanye West's albums and awards

By Luke Leonard

Kanye West is debatably one of the best music writers in the world. Kanye has made many great albums such as Graduation, Ye, Watch the Throne etc... Kanye has won 3 Grammy awards throughout his career.

Kanye's top five Best Albums

1. Watch the Throne (2011)

2. Yeezus (2013)

3. 808s & Heartbreak (2008)

4. The Life of Pablo (2016)

5. Graduation (2007)
